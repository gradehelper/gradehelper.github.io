The Series So Far

Season 1

The first season of Just Blood follows police officer Daniel as he battles for justice in the face of systemic failure and personal loss. Across 8 gripping episodes, the show unravels not just the mystery behind his sister Maggie’s murder, but also the psyche of a man losing faith in everything he once stood for.

Episode 1

A quiet day unfolds: case files, routine patrols with Liza, a call or five from Maggie. Nothing feels out of place until a knock at the door fractures everything. By midnight, the world Daniel knew is gone.

Episode 2

Daniel returns to work after Maggie’s funeral, tension brewing between him and the force. Liza is caught in the middle.

Episode 3

A lead in Maggie's murder surfaces but red tape and bureaucracy threaten to shut it down. Daniel’s frustration with the legal system begins to crack through his moral compass.

Episode 4

Anton is arrested, showing no remorse when Liza interrogates him. To uncover new truths, Daniel meets with Maggie’s old friend - now his father's secretary.

Episode 5

Daniel struggles with his mental health, hallucinations of Maggie haunting him. The police close ranks to protect a donor at the expense of one of their own.

Episode 6

Daniel is suspended after a violent outburst, seemingly uncharacteristic of him. Liza receives a call from an unfriendly face.

Episode 7

The trial begins. Daniel debates taking matters into his own hands. Liza has to make a choice.

Episode 8

Anton's verdict is handed down. Daniel’s face gives nothing away. Where does justice draw the line?
